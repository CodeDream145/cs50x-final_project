And here it is:

$ Write down what you want to do inside the dish table and add it to your stack of dishes.

$ The dish may be your goal or the thing you want to do today (only today). As this method focuses only on one day.

$ Name your dish and describe it clearly and briefly.

$ And eat it when you have done it.
